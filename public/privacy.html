Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 26, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Biblical Battle Plans, an RPG-style Bible reading tracker developed by ShirePath Solutions, LLC. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect when you use our web application and iOS mobile app, how we use that data, and your rights regarding your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address: Required for account creation and authentication
  • Username: Your unique identifier within the app
  • Display name: Optional name shown to other users
  • Avatar URL: Optional profile picture
  • Authentication provider: Whether you signed up with email/password or Google OAuth

Reading Activity Data

  • Daily reading progress: Chapters read, completion status, and timestamps
  • Bible reading plans: Which plan types you use (Sequential, Horner, 52-Week, Free Reading)
  • Streaks and achievements: Consecutive reading days, ranks earned, and shields acquired
  • Chapter history: Complete log of all chapters you've marked as read
  • Active campaigns: Your currently selected reading plan and progress

Guild Activity

  • Guild memberships: Guilds you've joined or created
  • Guild roles: Whether you're an owner, admin, or member
  • Reading activity within guilds: Your reading progress visible to guild members
  • Guild leaderboard data: Your rank and stats compared to other members

Device and Technical Data

  • Browser/device information: User agent, screen size, operating system
  • Session tokens: Stored in localStorage for authentication
  • Notification preferences: iOS notification permissions and reminder times
  • App version: Which version of the app you're using
  • Usage analytics: Page views, feature usage, and performance metrics (via Vercel Analytics)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Core Functionality

  • Authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • Track your daily Bible reading progress across plans
  • Calculate streaks, achievements, and rank progression
  • Enable guild features including leaderboards and activity feeds
  • Provide daily verse inspiration on your dashboard

Notifications and Reminders

  • Send daily reading reminder notifications on iOS devices (if enabled)
  • Cancel notifications when you've completed your daily reading
  • Respect your notification time preferences

Improvement and Support

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features and user experience
  • Monitor app performance and identify technical issues
  • Respond to support requests and feedback
  • Develop new features based on user behavior and requests

Third-Party Services

Biblical Battle Plans uses the following third-party services that may collect or process your data:

Supabase (Database & Authentication)

  • Purpose: Backend database, authentication, and real-time features
  • Data shared: All account, reading, and guild data
  • Security: Row Level Security (RLS) policies protect your data
  • Privacy Policy: supabase.com/privacy

Google OAuth (Optional)

  • Purpose: Alternative sign-in method
  • Data shared: Email address and basic profile info from Google
  • Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Vercel Analytics

  • Purpose: Privacy-friendly usage analytics and performance monitoring
  • Data collected: Page views, performance metrics, no personal identifiers
  • Privacy Policy: vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

Sentry (Optional Error Tracking)

  • Purpose: Error monitoring and debugging
  • Data shared: Error logs, stack traces, and browser information
  • Note: Currently optional and may not be active
  • Privacy Policy: sentry.io/privacy

Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data Is Stored

  • Cloud database: Supabase cloud infrastructure (PostgreSQL)
  • Local storage: Browser localStorage for session tokens and preferences
  • iOS device: Local notification scheduling data (iOS app only)

Security Measures

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Row Level Security (RLS) policies ensure users can only access their own data
  • Session tokens expire and require periodic re-authentication
  • API endpoints are protected with authentication requirements

Data Sharing and Disclosure

What's Visible to Other Users

When you join a guild, the following information is visible to other guild members:

  • Your username and display name
  • Your avatar (if set)
  • Your reading statistics (streaks, total chapters, rank)
  • Your recent reading activity within the guild
  • Your position on guild leaderboards

We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is only shared with third-party services as necessary to provide the app's functionality (as described in the Third-Party Services section).

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or if we believe such action is necessary to comply with legal obligations or protect our rights.

Your Rights and Choices

Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Profile page in the app. When you delete your account:

  • All your reading progress and activity data is permanently deleted
  • Your guild memberships are removed
  • Your achievements and streaks are erased
  • Guilds you own are transferred to another admin or deleted if you're the sole owner
  • This action is irreversible

Notification Control

You can manage notification preferences:

  • Enable or disable daily reading reminders on iOS
  • Set your preferred reminder time
  • Notifications are automatically canceled when you complete your daily reading

Data Access

You can view all your reading data, streaks, and achievements within the app. For a complete export of your data, please contact us at support@biblicalbattleplans.com.

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data is retained as long as your account exists
  • Deleted accounts: Data is immediately removed from our database
  • Analytics data: Aggregated, anonymized analytics may be retained for up to 24 hours
  • Backups: Database backups may retain deleted data for up to 30 days for disaster recovery purposes

Children's Privacy

Biblical Battle Plans is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us immediately at support@biblicalbattleplans.com.

International Users

Biblical Battle Plans is operated in the United States. If you are accessing the app from outside the United States, please be aware that your data may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers and third-party service providers are located.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy
  • Notify users via email or in-app notification
  • Provide a reasonable period for you to review the changes

Your continued use of Biblical Battle Plans after the effective date of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
